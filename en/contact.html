Frequently Asked Questions

What languages do your guides speak?

Our guides are fluent in English and Spanish. We also have guides available who speak German and Italian for specific tours. Please let us know your language preferences when booking.

How far in advance should I book a tour?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for standard tours, and 1 month ahead for custom or special interest tours. During high season (December-January and June-August), earlier bookings are advised.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For bookings made within Colombia, we also accept cash payments at our office in Neiva.

Can you arrange transportation from Bogotá to Huila?

Yes, we can arrange private transportation or assist with booking flight tickets from Bogotá to Neiva. This service can be added to any tour package for an additional fee.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 4 or more people. The exact discount depends on the tour and group size. Contact us for a personalized quote.
